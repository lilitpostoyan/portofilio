Why I'm a Designer now

Story

My passion for beauty and care for people has guided every step of my journey. Years in the beauty industry and leadership roles taught me how to understand individual needs-even the unspoken ones-and help people transform into the best version of themselves.

Today, I bring that same mindset into digital design: creating well-researched, user-centered experiences that simplify life, support real needs, and make everyday moments feel a little more beautiful.
